Ira Levin

Ira Levin is the author of several novels, including the one put on show here,
Rosemarie's Baby. He was an American novelist, playwright, and songwriter, most noted
for the aforementioned story, as well as several others, including The Stepford Wives,
The Boys from Brazil, A Kiss Before Dying, and several other works.
He died on the November 17, 2007, in Manhattan, New York, an accomplished writer of note.
