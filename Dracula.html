Dracula

This tale by Bram Stoker is as well-known as they come: the legendary origin story
of the modern-day vampire. The story follows a cast of characters,
ranging from a simple worker in a housing firm to a doctor of a mental institute,
through their diary entries as they encounter the fearsome creature
and struggle against, forces of darkness they barely comprehend, fighting
for their lives, and for the lives of those around them, in a mix of
stunning suspense as night falls, or even as morning arrives,
to heart-clenching excitement as each make their move.

The story stands the test of time magnificently, enough so that
this classic story has earned its place on the docket of books
that we here recommend ANY reader of horror encounters,
as it has not disappointed yet.
